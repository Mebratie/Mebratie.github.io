About Me

I am a researcher in applied mathematics with interests in mathematical modeling, dynamical systems, and computational methods. My research focuses on developing mathematical and computational approaches for understanding complex systems, with applications in biological systems and scientific discovery.

Research Interests

  • Dynamical systems and differential equations
  • Mathematical modeling of biological and complex systems
  • Machine learning for discovering conservation laws and governing equations
  • Structural identifiability and symmetry analysis of mathematical models
  • Symbolic computation and scientific computing
