PhD in Mathematics

I am a researcher in applied mathematics with interests in mathematical modeling, dynamical systems, computational methods, and machine learning. My current research lies at the intersection of machine learning, applied mathematics, and computational sciences, with a focus on developing data-driven approaches for discovering conservation laws of dynamical systems and advancing mathematical models and computational methods for studying biological and complex systems.

Research Interests

  • Dynamical systems and differential equations
  • Mathematical modeling of biological and complex systems
  • Machine learning for discovering conservation laws and governing equations
  • Identifiability, observability, and Symmetries of Differential Equations
  • Symbolic computation and scientific computing
